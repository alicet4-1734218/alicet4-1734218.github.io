How to Play Puyo Puyo

GamePlay

You have to match 3 or more color links of Puyo to erase it! The more chains, the better!

Multiplayer

You can play against a computer or a real person online or locally!

About

Puyo Puyo is a series of games that used to be called Puyo Pop. They were developed by Sonic Team and his many modes to play.

There is a story mode where you get introduced to the Heroes and Villains of Puyo Puyo. Voice Acting included!

The games became a major success and sold millions of copies and downloads! Get your game today in the shop!
